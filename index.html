About the School

Samarkand State School 40 is a public institution dedicated to academic excellence since 1985. We provide a balanced curriculum that nurtures both knowledge and character. Our teachers are committed to guiding each student toward success — in classes, in projects, and in life.

Our Values

  • Respect and kindness
  • Curiosity and critical thinking
  • Perseverance and responsibility

Our Students

  • Get into prestigious institutions in Uzbekistan and abroad
  • Have an average of B1–B2 English level
